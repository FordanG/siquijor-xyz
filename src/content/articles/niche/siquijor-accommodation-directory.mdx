## Booking Tips

### When to Book

| Period | Advance Notice |
|--------|----------------|
| Holy Week | 2-4 weeks |
| December-February | 1-2 weeks |
| March-May | 1 week |
| June-November | Walk-ins possible |

### Where to Book

**Online Platforms:**
- Booking.com (largest selection)
- Agoda (good for Asia)
- Airbnb (unique properties)

**Direct Booking:**
- Often cheaper (no platform fees)
- Call or email directly
- Facebook pages for some properties

### Payment

- **Cash:** Preferred by most budget/mid-range
- **Cards:** Accepted at resorts
- **GCash/Maya:** Increasingly accepted
- **Bank transfer:** Some properties accept

<Warning severity="info">
Many budget accommodations aren't listed on booking platforms. For the best rates and widest selection, contact properties directly or search their Facebook pages.
</Warning>
